- **Link:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22822v1

Here is a 3-sentence summary of the research abstract:

Researchers have developed a new open effective field theory (EFT) for non-Abelian gauge theories, which describes the interactions between a system and its environment in a local and gauge-covariant way. ### How many systems can be dephased before the quantum switch becomes causally definite?
- **Authors:** Yassine Benhaj, Kuntal Sengupta, Cyril Branciard
- **Link:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22807v1

Here is a 3-sentence summary of the research abstract:

Researchers investigated how much nonclassicality is required for a quantum process to exhibit causal nonseparability, a property that can provide advantages over traditional quantum circuits. They found that dephasing all systems or only keeping the future system undephased results in a causally separable process, but retaining any other system undephased can preserve causal nonseparability. This behavior was observed in both bipartite and multipartite quantum processes, specifically in quantum circuits with quantum control, highlighting the importance of nonclassicality in maintaining causal nonseparability.
