**Authors:** Jesse J. Osborne, Cheuk Yiu Wong, Jad C. Halimeh

- A unified framework is proposed for dynamical quantum phase transitions (DQPTs).
- Manifold DQPTs are governed by resonances within the initial state manifold.

### Estimating Green's functions with a robust quantum Arnoldi method

**Authors:** Jacob S. Nelson, Andrew B. Baczewski

- ROQAM preserves the upper-Hessenberg structure of projected matrices through orthogonal polynomials.

### Emergent heavy-tailed distributions from a Markovian random walk

**Authors:** Henrique S. Lima, Evaldo M. F. Curado

- A Markovian random walk exhibits heavy-tailed distributions due to a local, deterministic feedback mechanism.
- The walk's stationary state is a non-Gaussian, Lorentz-like distribution with power-law tails decaying as |x|^{-2}.
- A non-zero initial acceleration drives the walker away from the origin, enabling scale-free statistics and a normalizable distribution.

[Read paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22933)
