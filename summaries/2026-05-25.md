### Unified resonant-manifold framework for dynamical quantum phase transitions

**Authors:** Jesse J. Osborne, Cheuk Yiu Wong, Jad C. Halimeh

- A unified framework explains dynamical quantum phase transitions (DQPTs) through energy structure of product state configurations.
- Manifold DQPTs are governed by resonances within the initial state, while branch DQPTs involve resonances with a transitional manifold.
- Branch DQPTs' regularity is linked to the multiplicity of the transitional manifold.

[Read paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22915)

### Emergent heavy-tailed distributions from a Markovian random walk

**Authors:** Henrique S. Lima, Evaldo M. F. Curado

- A Markovian random walk with a deterministic step length function exhibits heavy-tailed distributions.
- The walk's stationary state is a Lorentz-like distribution with power-law tails decaying as |x|^{-2}.
- Non-zero initial acceleration drives the walker away from the origin, enabling scale-free statistics.
